Gay people shouldn't be banned from donating blood

Right now, any man who had sex with another man is banned from donating blood. Most LGBTQ organizations view these policies as based on homophobia rather than legitimate medical reasons.

What is happening right now

Currently, any man who had sex with men in the Philippines cannot donate blood. This is also the case in most countries such as the US, Canada, and Australia.

During the CoVid pandemic, a blood shortage happened. This wasn't uncommon though. Blood shortages happened many times in the past. When vast amounts of people are unable to donate blood, even if they wanted to help, they cannot.
